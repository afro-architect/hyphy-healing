About

Hyphy & Healing

Hyphy & Healing is a Bay Area–rooted project that treats joy, movement, and community care as real mental health tools for teens.

We highlight neighborhood classes, community centers, and youth‑led spaces where you can move your body, connect with friends, and feel seen—without needing perfect words, perfect outfits, or perfect vibes.

What This Site Is

This prototype is built like a teen‑friendly wellness magazine: part events calendar, part culture zine, and part resource hub designed with Bay Area students and educators in mind.

  • Stories about community‑based movement and mental health.
  • Roundups of free or low‑cost events across the Bay.
  • Example layouts for youth‑centered media projects and curriculum.

Who It’s For

Hyphy & Healing is for Bay Area teens, educators, and community organizers who want mental health resources that feel culturally grounded, joyful, and actually reachable after school or work.
